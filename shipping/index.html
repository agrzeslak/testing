SHIPPING

Is international delivery available?

Yes, we deliver everywhere, yay!

How long does production take?

For smaller items, approx. 2 weeks.
For side tables & large tables, approx. 3-4 weeks.

How long does delivery take?

For smaller items, approx. 2-3 weeks.
For large tables, approx. 3-4 weeks.

Refunds & exchanges?

No refunds or returns for change of mind after items have been shipped/picked up. Full refunds can be approved before the start of production. After safe delivery, refunds or returns are not accepted due to broken items or defects. Please message us to purchase a warranty before ordering.

Is there a warranty?

Delivery cost does not include a warranty. Damage/loss of items from transit is not covered. Damage of items or broken pieces during your ownership is not covered. Please message us to purchase a warranty before ordering.

How much is delivery for decor objects (lamps, trays, stands, & dishes)?

$15 for Australia. $20 for everywhere else.

How much is delivery for tables?

Prices are shown at checkout.

what does delivery include?

Delivery only includes unloading to your address (outside your property). Some packages cannot fit through your front gates, doors, or staircases. We always advise you to unpack outside and carry in the items yourself. Packages can weigh up to a ton. Total package size can be very large. We never advise our clients to bring the whole package inside. Tables are packaged with heavy wooden crates, plywood boards, foam, bubble wrap, plastic, & tightening straps. 

Is pickup available?

Pickups are not currently available.
